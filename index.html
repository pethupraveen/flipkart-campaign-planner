Optimizer rules & thresholds

The action plan is rules-based. Tune the thresholds to your margins and risk appetite. Defaults suit a mobile-accessories catalog (ASP ≈ ₹550).
How the rules fire, in priority order:
1. Pause / Fix listing — meaningful spend & clicks but zero units → price, stock, rating or wrong-keyword problem.
2. Pause / cut bid hard — ROAS below break-even on meaningful spend → losing money on every sale.
3. Reduce bid −10–15% — ROAS between break-even and target → profitable but below goal.
4. Scale +15–25% — ROAS well above target → raise bids/budget to buy more volume.
5. Improve content — big views, tiny CTR → thumbnail, title or price is the bottleneck (not the ad).
